Mining & Processing

ErfanianMining drives Afghanistan’s mineral industry by transforming raw mineral resources into high-quality, value-added products. Our integrated approach encompasses a full spectrum of operations—from comprehensive exploration and resource evaluation to efficient extraction, state-of-the-art processing, and refined product casting—ensuring every step boosts local employment, empowers communities, and positions Afghanistan favorably in global markets.

Our roadmap begins with advanced geological surveys and data-driven exploration techniques that accurately assess mineral potential. Once resource viability is confirmed, we implement precise drilling, blasting, and ore extraction using modern technologies. The extracted ore is meticulously crushed, milled, and enriched via flotation and leaching processes. We then refine the metal through electrowinning and cast it into premium ingots—all under strict quality control protocols utilizing advanced laboratory analyses.
